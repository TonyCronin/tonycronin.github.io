About me

Hi I’m Tony Cronin and I’m the UX (User Experience) Lead for PyracCloud. I build profitable applications, which Customers will buy and users will enjoy using. I’ve built applications for Enterprise, IOT and in latterly SaaS solutions.

I began life as a Graphic Designer, designing for traditional media, from about 1997 I began to build websites (in fact I remember when CSS was version 1), for myself and then for businesses. I began to use and build CMS systems - mostly WordPress then Drupal. This work-arc meant I had to hack at PHP, MYSQL and BASH a good deal, using a process of trial and mostly error. By using Stack Overflow I began to see that there where better and less time consuming workflows. I began to learn, using free resources like Codecademy and paid training like the now defunct CodesSchool (Rails for zombies anyone?), Treehouse, Edx and Coursera. All this training really whetted my appetite, I completed a Masters of Science in Computer Science in 2014 from the lovely University of Hertfordshire. I did my thesis on Cooper’s Personas, Rapid Prototyping and Angular.

I discovered Ruby and Rails in 2008. I came to a deep realization that the philosophy of a framework can direct the work you do, and that understanding the technology enriches the process and output of a UX professional. I also realized that great frameworks are usually the work of great vision, people like David Heinemeier Hansson or Matz.

I was lucky enough to hear about R from this guy in 2012. I now use R, and particularly the Tidyverse (mostly the vision and work of Hadley Wickham) to make data driven decisions about the problems our users face and how we can develop successfully against this evidence.

Education

  • Advanced Diploma Visual Communication. Graded 2.1. Dublin Institute of Technology. Specializing in Graphic Design and Typography.
  • MSc Computer Science. First Class Honors. Spent just over 4,000 hours (I know because I logged every hour as a consultant), getting quite good at Java, Python and JavaScript. I received a commendation from the Dean of Computer Science.

Work

  • CroninDesigns from 2004 to 2016, I was Director, Creative Director, Sales Person, Photographer, Web Developer (Mostly PHP and JavaScript), Server Admin, Process Designer, Shorts Maker and Head Coffee Maker. We survived the Great Irish Recession of 2007-2013, with a lot of hard work, user focus and realizing that polymaths have a special place in the world. I sold the company as a going concern in 2015.
  • Neosfar from 2015-2016, I was an interaction Designer (IxD) at Neosfar, an IoT startup. I used Carroll and Rosson’s lightweight Scenario Based Design to quickly roll out an application.
  • SoftwareONE since early 2016 I’m the User Experience Lead at PyracCloud. SoftwareONE is a very hardworking and creative place, with a excellent set of Core Values.
