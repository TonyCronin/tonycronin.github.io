About me

Hi I’m Tony Cronin and I’m the UX (User Experience) Lead for PyracCloud I build profitable applications, which Customers will buy and users will enjoy using. I’ve built applications for Enterprise, IOT and in the cloud.

I began life as a Graphic Designer, designing for traditional media, from about 1997 I began to build websites (in fact I remember when CSS was version 1), for myself and then for businesses. I began to use and build CMS systems - mostly WordPress then Drupal. This work-arc meant I had to hack at PHP, MYSQL and BASH a good deal, using a process of trial and mostly error. By using Stack Overflow I began to see that there where better and less time consuming workflows. I began to learn, using free resources like Codecademy and paid training like the now defunct CodesSchool (Rails for zombies anyone?), Treehouse, Edx and Coursera.
